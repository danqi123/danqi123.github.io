About Me.

Biomedical Data Scientist

"Curious about the world, and discovering the secret of data."

I got my first master's degree in Pharmacy (clinical proteomics domain), with 7+ years of working experience in wet biological labs.

I joined Fraunhofer SCAI - AI and Data Science team as a student research assistant in May 2022.

Now I am a team member of the AIOLOS Project, mainly in charge of generating an early ALERT model for the COVID-19 pandemic.

My research interest focuses on Information retrieval, Time series data engineering, and NLP in Biomedical domain.
